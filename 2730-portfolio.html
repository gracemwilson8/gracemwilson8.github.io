Welcome to my "Digital Writing" Portfolio!

Hi! My name is Grace Wilson and I am a second year English major and Journalism minor at Northeastern University.

This site includes a variety of digital based writing pieces I made for my class, Digital Writing. While in my heart I imagine myself as a sitting-by-candle-light-writing-poetry-on-a-scroll type of writer, this class taught me all about the world of digital writing, and I am happy to say I enjoyed it much more than I expected. While I had to trade impractical and fast-burning candlelight for the convenience of a laptop with a relatively strong battery life, I still found ways to weave my creativity in every assignment. I learned that digital writing is just one way I can exercise my creativity in the future. Enjoy!
